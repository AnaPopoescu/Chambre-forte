Did you asked yourself what are the activities that makes your heart beats faster?


 I will tell you some of them so you can practice outside 🆚 inside the house depending on what you like!

 Let's start with the indoor activities 🆒


 Whether it’s due to bad weather ⚡, an unexpected school closure 🎓, or some other reason, once you’re indoors,you don't have to waste your energy 🌞 because there are so many funny things you can do

For exemple, even it's the oldest idea in the book, but if you really want some screen-free family time 💝, old-fashioned board games still do the trick. Get your competitive spirit up and get ready to play 📴 All you need is some popcorn 🍿, juice 🍹 and much fun!!

×

But there are more than just these games: you can diy your own masterpieces if you like to make something homemade OR it's a useful idea to bake and decorate the cookies/brownies 🍪 because indoor days are the perfect time to try and get creative in the kitchen.🥐 Whip up some kind of make-your-own dessert bar by putting out toppings (frosting, sprinkles, M&Ms). 🥜

               

And one of the funniest activity is DANCE!! Choose a playlist and move on the beat.💃

   

Others ideas are learn a new skill, make a puzzle, play with the science, read a book, make a hunt, play karaoke 🎤 or if you like video games, try a new and productive one!


               

🆕 There are also the outdoor activities and fortunately you can choose from a much wider range 🎨

Depending on your area, there are likely some outdoor activities 🌳 you can safely partake in, which can help give you a much-needed mental reset

You can start with basic sports, for example the next sports: volleyball 🏐, baseball 🏈, football 🥅, badminton 🎾, basketball 🏀, bowling 🎳, cycling , tennis 🏓. There are always a good choice and you can enjoy it with your friends because these sports require a larger group of people.

           

But if you are more like a nature person you can try paintball (it's a good sport also if you like to paint or art in general) or camping with the loved ones 💑. A good walk in nature is so refreshing even it's simple activity. And for those who love the land or want to really feel it, a good activity gardening especially because you get rid of stress and you can grow your own fruits 🍒/ vegetables 🍅


     

 If you are more like an adventurous person 🤸 and you don't have the feeling to control the situation, a good outdoor activity is swimming 🏊, ice climbing, surfing 🌊, canoeing, skiing 🎿 etc.🏄
